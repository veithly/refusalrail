RefusalRail logo system

Six SVG directions generated for a forensic RWA policy workbench. The selected mark is Rail Gate: a policy stop placed directly on the execution rail.

Rail Gate

Two execution rails meet a visible stop. It scales cleanly to a favicon and reads as product-specific rather than generic security.

Ledger Spine

Receipt rows attach to a durable ledger spine. The mark is trustworthy but not as retellable as Rail Gate.
