Top 3 most demoable / most memorable picks

1. IDEA-010 Refusal Theater
This is the strongest demo package. The premise is immediately understandable: “this demo wins only if the trade fails.” The judge directly chooses the failure condition, the contract visibly refuses, and the minted refusal receipt gives a concrete artifact. It is also easy to retell: “The contract stamped NO on a live trade.”

2. IDEA-007 T+60 Ghost Order
Most theatrical and probably the most likely to be remembered by name. The countdown creates tension, and the judge’s injected shock changes the outcome inside the demo window. Slightly less product-clear than RecallRail or ProofPay, but much more surprising.

3. IDEA-006 RecallRail
The clearest real-world problem: money went to the wrong address. The opt-in recall rail is understandable, interactive, and visibly consequential. It has less stage magic than Ghost Order, but more obvious user value.

Selected winner from this judge

Winner: IDEA-010 Refusal Theater

It best matches the hackathon demo constraints: immediate first click, judge-controlled outcome, visible onchain consequence, proof artifact, and a one-sentence rumor. It is not the deepest financial primitive, but it is the most judge-proof demo.
