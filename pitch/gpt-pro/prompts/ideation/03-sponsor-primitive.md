你是 HackathonHunter 的外部深度推理顾问。请为以下黑客松生成 exactly 3 个高质量项目 idea。你的视角是 sponsor primitive：Robinhood Chain / Arbitrum / ZeroDev / Dune / OpenZeppelin / GMX / Fhenix 等赞助或生态技术必须成为产品机制，而不是 logo。

- Required deployment: an Arbitrum chain. Supported submission networks include Arbitrum One, Arbitrum Nova, Robinhood Chain testnet, and Arbitrum Sepolia.
- Prize strategy:

- Sponsor primitives:
  - Robinhood Chain: tokenized RWA / equities / ETF / private asset context, self-custody, programmatic finance, 24/7 access, Arbitrum Chain tech.
