# Draft Pitch

## Slide 1 - Reject 1 unsafe RWA agent trade in 60 seconds.

RefusalRail rejects one unsafe RWA agent trade in 60 seconds.

The judge chooses a shock, the agent attempts a principal sale, and the policy rail refuses it. The result is not a warning banner. It is a receipt with policy hash, calldata hash, shock hash, owner, reason, and proof hash.

The safe contrast matters: the same policy can allow a distribution sweep while refusing principal sale under halt, stale price, or exposure breach.
